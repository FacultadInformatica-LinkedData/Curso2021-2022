Defining our Resource Naming Strategy

First of all, since our datasets are small, we are going to use hash URIs.
The URI domain that we are going to use is: 

  • http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/

Next, we are going to have 4 subfolders, one for each dataset:

  • http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/accidentes/
  • http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/infantiles/
  • http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/mayores/
  • http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/barrios/


In our ontology, we will have different forms for our classes,properties and instances. These forms are: 

  • Classes: http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/[subfolder]/ontology/<ontologyName>#<className>
  • Properties: http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/[subfolder]/ontology/<ontologyName>#<propertyName>
  • Instances: http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/[subfolder]/resource/<className>#<identifier>
