Requirements for our datasets.

These are the requirements followed by our datasets.


R1. Data are in one of the selected smart city domains.

Our dataset takes place in Barcelona, which is said one of the best smart cities around the world.:
Accidentes de tráfico con implicación de bicicletas
Transporte en Barcelona

R2. Data are available as a CSV file.

The datasets are in CSV.

R3. Data have an open license so they can be published.

The general conditions allow the reuse of documents for commercial and non-commercial means. More information is available at > License.

R4. Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities (e.g., locations).

The fields of the datasets can be linked with generic real-world entities such as transport in Barcelona.

R5. Documentation exists for the data (optional).

If there is any documentation available it could be found through the link provided at the requirement one (1).

R6. Data can come from multiple data sources (optional).

There are plenty of data sources that has access to the public transports in Barcelona.
